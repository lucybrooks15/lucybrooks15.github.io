About Me

My name is Lucy Brooks. I grew up in Mangawhai, a small beach town up north. This really allowed me to appreciate the environment and the creative  culture there. Moving to Auckland 6 years ago let me expand on this as the city was very different culturally and had more opportunities. Painting and drawing has always been a passion of mine since I was young and I started to understand and love design in high school.
